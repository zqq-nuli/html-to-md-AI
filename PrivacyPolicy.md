Privacy Policy / 隐私权政策

[English]
This Privacy Policy applies to the Markdown converter browser extension.
1. No Data Collection: This extension operates entirely locally on your device. We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any of your personal data, browsing history, or the content of the web pages you visit.
2. Local Processing: All conversions from web page content to Markdown are performed locally within your browser. No data is ever sent to any external servers.
3. Permissions: The extension requests necessary permissions (such as activeTab, scripting, and host permissions) solely for the purpose of accessing the current page's Document Object Model (DOM) to perform the text extraction and conversion. These permissions are used strictly to deliver the core functionality of the extension.
4. Contact: If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact [admin@035616.click].

4. 联系方式：如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请联系 [admin@035616.click]。
